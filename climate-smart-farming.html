Climate-Smart Farming

Climate-smart farming practices help farmers adapt to climate change while increasing productivity and sustainability.

What is Climate-Smart Farming?

Climate-smart farming includes practices that reduce greenhouse gas emissions, improve resilience to climate impacts, and enhance farm productivity.

Best Practices for Climate-Smart Farming

  • Adopt conservation agriculture techniques like minimum tillage and cover cropping.
  • Optimize water use with drip irrigation and rainwater harvesting.
  • Plant climate-resilient crop varieties.
  • Use renewable energy on farms, such as solar-powered irrigation systems.
