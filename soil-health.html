Soil Health and Management

Good soil health is critical for sustainable agriculture. Learn how to improve soil structure, fertility, and biodiversity.

What is Soil Health?

Soil health refers to the soil's ability to function as a living ecosystem, supporting plant growth, water filtration, and nutrient cycling. Healthy soils are critical for long-term agricultural productivity.

Best Practices for Managing Soil Health

  • Use crop rotation to improve soil fertility.
  • Incorporate organic matter like compost and manure.
  • Test soil regularly to monitor pH, nutrient levels, and organic content.
  • Practice no-till or reduced-till farming to reduce soil erosion.
